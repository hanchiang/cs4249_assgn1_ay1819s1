Instructions

You will be given 18 tasks to complete for 3 rounds, which amounts to a total of 54 trials.

Task

Select the item specified under the column Please select.

Click the Next button after you have made your selection

Two types of menu

1. Marking Menu

Hold the right mouse button to reveal the menu and move the cursor to the item. You may make a selection without waiting for the menu to appear.

2. Radial Menu

Click the right mouse button to reveal the menu, and click left mouse button to select the item.

After each round

Take a break of 30 seconds after each of the 3 rounds
